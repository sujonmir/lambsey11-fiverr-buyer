The Smallish Studio is a micro creative agency in London, founded by creative director Lisa Clarke. Small is beautiful — with over 25 years in interiors and lifestyle, I create content that performs. From full campaign direction to photography, copy, consultancy, and audits, I flex to what your brand needs.

About

Rooted in editorial with commercial edge, The Smallish Studio specialises in interiors and lifestyle brands. I've built a global network of talent — stylists, photographers, writers, and AI experts — to deliver creative that works across print, digital, and all platforms. Every project benefits from insight, detail, and meticulous attention.

How I Work

You get me on every project. I start by getting under the skin of your brand and understanding your goals. Then I shape the concept, assemble the right team, and direct each stage — from idea to delivery. Bespoke, responsive, and built for results.

From concept to delivery – strategic consultancy, creative direction, or complete project management. Working across all platforms: stills, motion, print, and digital.
